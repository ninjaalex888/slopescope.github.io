Checkout

VR ECommerce Marketplace

About

We are in the process of creating Checkout, a virtual reality shopping experience. We plan to develop this application for popular HMD virtual reality devices like the Oculus Rift and the HTC Vive. Our goal is to revolutionize the online shopping experience by adding an additional degree of immersion from the typical 2d online shopping format.

We believe that VR shopping can improve the traditional online shopping experience by making the process more interactive and by improving the online representation of products.

Checkout aims to provide the following key features and benefits:

  • A realistic shopping experience that doesn't require getting ready or leaving your house
  • Ability to talk to friends and other shoppers about products
  • 360-degree view allowing for a better understanding of appearance, colors, and dimensions
  • Better idea of clothing fit
  • Experience stores and products not available where you live
